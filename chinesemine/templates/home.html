Welcome to Chinese Mine

Chinese Mine is a website that helps you learn Mandarin through sentence mining. Sentence mining is a method of language learning that involves finding and memorizing sentences that contain words and grammar patterns that you want to learn. By doing this, you can learn Mandarin in a natural and contextual way, without relying on boring textbooks and grammar rules.

With Chinese Mine, you can create and review flashcards for words, text sentences, and audio/visual sentences using our SRS app. SRS stands for spaced repetition system, which is a technique that helps you remember things better by showing them to you at optimal intervals. By using our SRS app, you can learn Mandarin faster and more efficiently, without forgetting what you have learned.

Whether you are a beginner or an advanced learner, Chinese Mine can help you improve your Mandarin skills and achieve your language goals. You can choose from a variety of topics and levels, and customize your flashcards according to your preferences. You can also track your progress, compete with other users, and earn rewards as you learn.

Ready to start your Mandarin learning journey? Join Chinese Mine today and discover the power of sentence mining!

Why Choose Chinese Mine?

Word Flashcards

Learn the most common and useful words in Mandarin with our word flashcards. You can see the hanzi, pinyin, and meaning of each word, and listen to the pronunciation. You can also create your own word flashcards with the words that you want to learn.

Text Sentence Flashcards

Learn how to use words in context with our text sentence flashcards. You can see the hanzi, pinyin, and meaning of each sentence, and listen to the pronunciation. You can also create your own text sentence flashcards with the sentences that you want to learn.

Audio/Visual Sentence Flashcards

Learn how to understand and speak Mandarin with our audio/visual sentence flashcards. You can watch and listen to authentic Mandarin content from various sources, such as YouTube videos, podcasts, and movies. You can also create your own audio/visual sentence flashcards with the content that you want to learn.
